About Us

Coding Autism trains and helps autistic individuals find jobs in the software and technology industries. We do so by linking up autistic individuals with software engineering and coding educational programs as well as conduct resume workshops, job application construction and revision, interview preparation, and mentorship.

Our Services

Training

We partner with coding bootcamps to proide a educational experience tailored to the needs of young adults on the autism spectrum.

Mentorship/Advocacy

We provide guidance and direction to our members. Skilled counsellors establish workplace standards and ensure that partnered companies maintain those high standards.

Resources

We provide resume and job preperation training, and career counseling.

Networking

We provide a growing network of alumni and mentors in the tech space.
